# Skill: Vietnam Banking Regulatory

Regulatory context for banking and financial services in Vietnam.

## MUST — Guardrails

- **Never fabricate SBV circular numbers or decree references.** If uncertain, flag it and recommend verification.
- **Always flag when regulation knowledge may be outdated.** SBV issues circulars frequently. Note the knowledge date.
- **Use real-time search for specific regulatory questions.** Do not rely on training data alone.
- **Distinguish the regulatory hierarchy** (same as insurance — Luật > Nghị định > Thông tư > Quyết định).

## SHOULD — Frameworks

### Regulatory Landscape

- **Primary laws**:
  - Law on Credit Institutions (Luật Các tổ chức tín dụng)
  - Law on the State Bank of Vietnam (Luật Ngân hàng Nhà nước Việt Nam)
  - Law on Anti-Money Laundering (Luật Phòng, chống rửa tiền)
  - Law on Electronic Transactions (Luật Giao dịch điện tử)
- **Regulatory body**: State Bank of Vietnam (Ngân hàng Nhà nước Việt Nam — SBV/NHNN)
- **Key regulatory areas**:
  - Capital adequacy and Basel framework adoption (Basel II → Basel III transition)
  - Credit risk classification and provisioning
  - Payment systems and e-payment regulations
  - KYC/eKYC requirements
  - AML/CFT obligations and reporting (suspicious transaction reports)
  - Interest rate and lending regulations
  - Foreign exchange controls
  - Outsourcing and third-party risk management
  - Technology risk management and cybersecurity
  - Open banking and API standards (emerging)
  - Data localization and cross-border data transfer

### Bancassurance Context

Where insurance and banking intersect:
- Bancassurance distribution regulations
- Mis-selling prevention requirements
- Commission and fee disclosure
- Customer suitability assessment
- Bundled product restrictions

## COULD — Open Space

- **Basel III adoption trajectory.** Vietnam is in transition. How far along is adoption, and what's the timeline for remaining requirements? This affects capital planning and system requirements.
- **Digital banking evolution.** Vietnam is moving toward digital banking licenses and open banking. Flag where current regulations may soon change.
- **Cross-border implications.** For insurance companies with banking partnerships, where do banking regulations create constraints or opportunities for insurance distribution?
- **Fintech regulatory sandbox.** Vietnam has experimented with sandbox approaches. Is the topic eligible for sandbox treatment?
